The Grayson Utilities, through its consultant, Bell Engineering, will be conducting Smoke Testing of the Sanitary Sewers in the area along the west side of Carol Malone Blvd from College Street north to KY 1947.  This area includes Christian Drive, Landsdowne Avenue north of College Street, Academic Pkwy, McClave St, and Everman St, beginning on December 17, 2020.
