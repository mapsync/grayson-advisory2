**7th STREET ROAD CLOSED ANNOUNCEMENT                                                                                                                 For Friday, January 16th, 2026**


On Friday, January 16th, 7th Street will be closed for utility line relocation work.
The closure will be at the entrance of 7th Street and KY1 South.
The road will close at 8:30 AM and will remain closed for the duration of the
business day. No service outages are planned.
We apologize for any inconvenience this may cause. The Utility Commission
advises motorists to use alternate routes.
