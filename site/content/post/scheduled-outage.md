A scheduled outage is planned for Thursday, October 5th. The areas affected will be Robert & Mary Ave from 3rd St to 5th St and Rupert Lane from 5th St to the Bowling Alley and all side streets. The water will be off Thursday morning at 9:00 for maintenance upgrades. Customers in this area will experience low to no pressure and will be under a Boil Water Advisory once water is restored.
