Thursday, May 15th, the waterline work will continue at 8 AM at the intersection of 2nd Street & Landsdowne Ave. This intersection will be closed to traffic. The water service will remain on during this time.

Beginning at 12PM, Noon, a service outage is planned for Hord Street, starting at Main Street to the end of North Hord Street. This outage will also include 4th Street from Hord Street to Pomeroy Street. Water Service will be restored by the end of the business day. Those affected customers will be on a Boil Water Advisory until further notice.

During the Service outage Hord Street will be closed at the intersections of 2nd Street and Hord Street as well as 3rd Street and Hord Street. 

The Utility Commission advises motorists to use alternate routes.

The Utility Commission asks motorists to be aware of the work zone, expect delays, and utilize safe driving habits when traveling through the area.

The date, time, and duration of work may be adjusted if inclement weather or other unforeseen delays occur.
