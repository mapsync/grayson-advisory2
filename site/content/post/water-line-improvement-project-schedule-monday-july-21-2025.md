On Monday, July 21st, at 12:00, Noon the water service will be off at the Carter County Courthouse, the 400 Block of West 2nd Street, 211-308 Malone Lane, Pine Street, Hall Street, and North Hill Street to make a tie-in connection to the new water line.  The road will be temporarily closed at the intersection of West 2nd Street and North Hill Street for the completion of the tie-in.  

Water service will be restored by the end of the business day.  Customers affected by this outage will be on a boil water advisory. Projected lift date of Wednesday, July 23rd.

The Utility Commission advises motorists to use alternate routes.

The Utility Commission asks motorists to be aware of the work zone, expect delays, and utilize safe driving habits when traveling through the area.

The date, time, and duration of work may be adjusted if inclement weather or other unforeseen delays occur.
