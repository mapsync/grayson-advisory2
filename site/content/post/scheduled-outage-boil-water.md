There will be a scheduled outage starting at 9am on Sept. 15th. The outage will be from Wolf Creek to the end of the line heading south along HWY 7 and HWY 1661. This will include all side roads, Baiercliff Rd, Landsacpe Dr, and Hackberry Ridge.  There will be a boil water advisory when service is restored.
