The Waterline Replacement work will have a temporary closure of the intersection on Hord Street and 3rd Street on Monday. Throughout the week, the work will continue on 3rd Street, West towards Landsdowne Ave.  This will include the intersection of Landsdowne Ave and 3rd St. This may cause a temporary closure of the intersection later in the week. When that section is completed, work will resume at 2 different locations for line tie ins. The first location will be behind Little Caeser's. The second location will be at the Life Center for the church on Pomeroy St. Notification will be provided ahead of any planned outages in those areas.

The Utility Commission advises motorists to use alternate routes.

The Utility Commission asks motorists to be aware of the work zone, expect delays, and utilize safe driving habits when traveling through the area.

The date, time, and duration of work may be adjusted if inclement weather or other unforeseen delays occur.
