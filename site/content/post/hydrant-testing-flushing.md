On Tuesday, January 18th, Hydrant testing and flushing will begin in the Leatherwood, KY 784 (Three Prong) and along KY 2 at Kehoe in Greenup County, along with areas along the AA Hwy including Friends Branch, Hurricane Branch and on KY 7 near the Iron Hill Church.

This may cause discoloration and/or low water pressure. Care should be taken wihile washing laundry during this time.

The Utiliies Commission apologizes for any inconvenience this may cause.
