Thursday, May 29th water line tie-ins will take place on Pomeroy Street, Main Street Alley, and at the intersection of Second Street and Pomeroy.  The water will be off starting at 10am for the following customers:

\*\**   100-166 East Main Street and 104-210 East Second Street

The water service will be restored by the end of the business day.  Once restored the customers will be on a Boil Water Advisory. 

The Utility Commission advises motorists to use alternate routes.

The Utility Commission asks motorists to be aware of the work zone, expect delays, and utilize safe driving habits when traveling through the area.

The date, time, and duration of work may be adjusted if inclement weather or other unforeseen delays occur.
