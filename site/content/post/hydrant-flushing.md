The Grayson Utilities Commission will flush fire hydrants starting on Monday, April 11th through Friday, April 29th, 2022. All areas will be affected.

This may cause discoloration and or low water pressure.

The Utilities Commission apologizes for any inconvenience this may cause. care should be taken while washing laundry during this time.

For any question, please call the office at 474-7569.
