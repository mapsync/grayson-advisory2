Beginning at 8:30 AM  on Friday, August 6th, Landsdowne Avenue at the Intersection of 2nd St will be CLOSED  for a Water Line Leak Repair.  The road will be re-opened by 4:00 PM. This closure will allow for the street surface to be removed. Another closure for the repairs will occur next week.  Another Announcement will be made prior to the second closure.
